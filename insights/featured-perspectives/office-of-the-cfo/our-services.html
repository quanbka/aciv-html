Our Solutions

FTI Consulting’s Office of the CFO Solutions Is Dedicated to Helping CFOs

OCFO Solutions
View Presentation

CFOs in every sector and industry are facing increased challenges and demands from boards of directors, stakeholders and other internal and external sources. To do their jobs successfully, deliver on these new demands and drive enterprise value, today’s CFOs must be able to balance traditional finance responsibilities with focus on strategy, performance and operations, in addition to managing risk, talent and stakeholders. FTI Consulting’s Office of the CFO Solutions helps CFOs drive business strategy and deliver forceful results.



Strategy & Planning

To help you create value for your organization and enhance its decision-making capabilities, we implement solutions that build confidence, clarity, controls and consistency. Enhanced by innovative engagement tools, we offer you strategies and solutions to drive value and improve risk management:

Planning and Performance Management

  • Performance Reporting and Analytics
  • Enterprise Performance Management (EPM)

Finance Function Strategy
