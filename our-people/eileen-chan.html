Eileen Chan is a Managing Director in the Corporate Finance & Restructuring practice of FTI Consulting and is based in Perth, Australia.

Eileen has over 25 years of experience in corporate advisory including IPO services, auditing, mergers and acquisitions as well as forensic accounting services. She has international audit experience for a wide portfolio of companies, including those listed in Hong Kong, China, Singapore and the U.S with operations in China.

Eileen was previously the CFO for an ASX-listed automotive technology company prior to joining FTI Consulting in 2013.

Prior to being a CFO, Eileen was an Audit Partner for a major international accounting firm in Hong Kong where she led the firm's Transactional Services Division, which principally focused on providing buy-side due diligence and transaction services. Eileen also led the Matrimonial Advisory Division, providing forensic accounting services in matrimonial dispute cases.

Eileen has specialist industry expertise in mining and mining services, civil earthmoving, the hospitality industry, pharmacies, professional services and retail industry.

Significant Engagements:

  • Kingsrose Mining Limited – trade on management of the ASX listed company with gold and silver mining operation in Indonesia under voluntary administration. Eileen was the acting CFO of the group responsible for the monthly reporting to the Administrators, quarterly and half yearly financial reporting to ASX including dealing with auditors and tax advisors and liaison with the ASX in respect of the relisting of the company. The company has been successfully turnaround, now returned to management and relisted on the ASX.
  • Kagara Ltd – trade on and asset sale management during the over 5 years administration and liquidation of the ASX listed base metal mining group with 3 mining projects in Northern Queensland and an exploration project in Western Australia. Acting as the CFO of the group, Eileen managed the monthly reporting, tax compliance and continuous disclosure obligations to the ASX.
  • Compass Hotel Group – trade on management of the receivership of the ASX listed hospitality group with 12 hotel venues in Western Australia. Eileen was responsible for the group’s financial reporting and tax compliance as well as performance of sell side due diligence during the sales process.
